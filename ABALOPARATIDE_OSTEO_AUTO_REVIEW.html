Audit trail

This review was generated by the audit-first topic-build pipeline. Each trial was verified at extraction time (not after) against the following gates:

  • GATE-A: NCT exists in AACT 2026-04-12 snapshot
  • GATE-B: Drug pattern in AACT interventions
  • GATE-C: Condition pattern in AACT conditions
  • GATE-D: Primary PMID's PubMed title+abstract contains drug OR condition
  • GATE-E: AACT baseline_counts gives ≥2 per-arm rows
  • GATE-F: AACT design_outcomes declares a primary outcome

All 3 trials in this review passed all 6 gates. Full per-trial gate results at outputs/new_topics/ABALOPARATIDE_OSTEO_AUTO.json.
