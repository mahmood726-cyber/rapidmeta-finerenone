Methodology headline

Engine v0.3.0 closed the v0.1/v0.2 diagonal-PM-per-dimension τ² approximation divergence to R mixmeta on RCS non-linearity Wald p:

Fixturev0.2 diagonal-PM pv0.3 full-REML pR mixmeta p|Δ| vs R
GL-1992 (k=8)≈ 0.050.70350.70690.0034
SGLT2i HbA1c (k=3)(not estimable)0.22710.22930.0022
Tirzepatide SURPASS (k=5)(not built yet)0.03460.03640.0018
Tirzepatide SURMOUNT (k=2)(not built yet)0.07590.07880.0029

The diagonal approximation flipped the qualitative non-linearity verdict on GL-1992 from "significant" (p ≈ 0.05) to "no evidence of non-linearity" (p ≈ 0.70) — a 14-fold p-value shift driven entirely by the τ² estimator structure. Off-diagonal between-study covariance carries the linear/non-linear correlation that diagonal approximations discard.
