# PMID Verification Cards

Generated by `scripts/generate_pmid_verification_cards.py` from
`outputs/pmid_corrections.csv`. 110 cards covering rows that
the auto-propose pass could not resolve unambiguously.

## How to use

Each card shows the dashboard's current claim (wrong PMID), what
PubMed says about that PMID, the top auto-search candidates with
their scores, and a link to search PubMed manually. Tick one box.
Once a card is decided, record the decision in
`outputs/pmid_verification_decisions.csv` (template provided)
and re-run `scripts/apply_pmid_corrections.py` after expanding it
to read the decisions CSV.

## Status legend

- **PROBABLE** — top auto-search candidate has a decent score but
  margin over runner-up was too small for safe auto-apply. Most
  of these are right; just need a quick eyeball on the title.
- **AMBIGUOUS** — multiple candidates have similar scores; pick by hand.
- **NO_PUBMED_HITS** — esearch returned 0; snippet may name the
  trial in a way PubMed doesn't index, or the trial published in a
  non-PubMed venue.
- **INSUFFICIENT_SNIPPET** — snippet had no author/year for auto-search.
