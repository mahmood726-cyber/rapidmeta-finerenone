# DOAC NMA vs LMWH/Warfarin for Acute VTE Treatment

**Version:** 1.0 · **Frozen:** 2026-04-21

## PICO

- Population: Adults with acute VTE (DVT, PE, or both)
- Interventions: Dabigatran 150 BID, Rivaroxaban 15 BID -> 20 QD, Apixaban 10 BID -> 5 BID, Edoxaban 60 QD
- Comparator: LMWH lead-in then Warfarin (INR 2-3)
- Outcome: Recurrent VTE or VTE-related death; HR

## Results (k=4, warfarin-star, tau2=0)

- Rivaroxaban: HR 0.68 (0.44, 1.04) - SUCRA 0.798
- Apixaban:    HR 0.84 (0.60, 1.18) - SUCRA 0.614
- Edoxaban:    HR 0.89 (0.70, 1.13) - SUCRA 0.572
- Dabigatran:  HR 1.10 (0.65, 1.85) - SUCRA 0.356

All DOACs non-inferior to LMWH/Warfarin. No DOAC statistically superior on recurrent-VTE alone. Net-benefit caveat (apixaban major-bleeding RR 0.31 vs warfarin in AMPLIFY) declared.
