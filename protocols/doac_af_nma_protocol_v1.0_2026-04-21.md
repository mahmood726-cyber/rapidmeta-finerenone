# DOAC NMA vs Warfarin for Stroke Prevention in Non-Valvular AF (RE-LY / ROCKET-AF / ARISTOTLE / ENGAGE-AF)

**Version:** 1.0 · **Frozen:** 2026-04-21 · **Author:** Mahmood Ahmad

## PICO

| | |
|---|---|
| Population | Adults with non-valvular atrial fibrillation and at least moderate stroke risk |
| Interventions | Dabigatran 150 mg BID · Rivaroxaban 20 mg QD · Apixaban 5 mg BID · Edoxaban 60 mg QD |
| Comparator | Warfarin (INR 2–3) |
| Outcome | Stroke or systemic embolism (trial-primary; HR) |

## Results (k=4, Warfarin-star)

| Drug | HR vs Warfarin (95% CI) | SUCRA |
|---|---:|---:|
| Dabigatran 150 BID | 0.66 (0.53, 0.82) | 0.923 |
| Apixaban 5 BID | 0.79 (0.66, 0.95) | 0.484 |
| Rivaroxaban 20 QD | 0.79 (0.66, 0.96) | 0.489 |
| Edoxaban 60 QD | 0.79 (0.63, 0.99) | 0.496 |

**Caveat on ranking:** Stroke/SE only. Net-benefit including major bleeding ranks Apixaban #1 (Ruff 2014 Lancet).
