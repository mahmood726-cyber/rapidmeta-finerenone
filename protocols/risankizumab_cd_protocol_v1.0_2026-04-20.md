## 2. PICO

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Population** | Adults with moderate-to-severe Crohn's disease (CDAI 220-450) inadequately responding to conventional or biologic therapy |
| **Intervention** | Risankizumab 600 mg IV induction at weeks 0/4/8; maintenance 360 mg SC Q8W |
| **Comparator** | Matched placebo (induction and maintenance) |
| **Outcome (primary)** | Clinical remission (CDAI <150) at the trial-specific primary timepoint (week 12 induction; week 52 maintenance) |

---

## 3. Eligibility Criteria

### Inclusion
- RCT (parallel, double-blind)
- Phase III pivotal trials in moderate-to-severe CD
- Risankizumab at licensed regimen (induction 600 mg IV; maintenance 360 mg SC Q8W)
- Placebo comparator
- Clinical remission and/or endoscopic response reported as co-primary
- Follow-up spanning induction (12 wk) or maintenance (52 wk)

### Exclusion
- Ulcerative colitis trials (separate JAK_UC review)
- Psoriasis or psoriatic arthritis trials (separate IL23_PSORIASIS review)
- Single-arm phase 2 risankizumab-CD studies (without parallel placebo)

## 6. Data Extraction

Study IDs, prior-biologic strata, CDAI baseline, clinical remission counts, endoscopic response counts, CRP/calprotectin, durable response at 52 wk, RoB 2.

---

## 7. Risk of Bias Assessment

Cochrane **RoB 2**. Design-based priors: LOW across D1-D5 (double-blind, blinded central endoscopy adjudication, SAP locked).

---

## 8. Synthesis / Statistical Methods

- **Primary pool:** DerSimonian-Laird random-effects IVW on log-OR scale for clinical remission.
- **CI adjustment:** HKSJ with t-distribution df = k-1.
- **Prediction interval:** t df = k-2 (Higgins 2009).
- **Subgroup / meta-regression:** Biologic-naive (ADVANCE broad-spectrum) vs biologic-failure (MOTIVATE) vs maintenance (FORTIFY re-randomized responders).
- **Sensitivity:** Leave-one-out; induction-only pool (ADVANCE + MOTIVATE) as primary if trial phase heterogeneity is high.
