RapidMeta Cardiology Portfolio

Living meta-analysis apps — 69 therapeutic topics, validated against published pools

Mahmood Ahmad · Pairwise · Updated 2026-04-20

Browser-based living meta-analysis apps spanning 69 therapeutic topics across cardiology (heart failure, renal, lipid, antiplatelet, diabetes CVOT), neurology (stroke thrombolysis + thrombectomy, MS, AD, migraine, hATTR), oncology (breast, prostate, lung, haematology), gastroenterology, hepatology, respiratory, dermatology, endocrinology, infectious disease, vaccinology, rheumatology, ophthalmology, psychiatry, and women's health. Each app ships a full Protocol → Search → Screening → Extraction → Analysis → Scientific Output pipeline, with auto-seeded canonical trials and pooled estimates cross-checked against published meta-analyses.

Every app tile below opens the full RapidMeta workbench — click into Screening to see Included / Excluded decisions, Extraction for 2×2 tables, and Analysis Suite for forest plots, Bayesian posterior, and PRISMA 2020 flow. Each app links to its frozen PROSPERO-style protocol (v1.0, frozen 2026-04-19) in the header toolbar.
