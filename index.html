RapidMeta Cardiology Portfolio

Living meta-analysis apps — 19 drug classes, validated against published pools

Mahmood Ahmad · Pairwise · Updated 2026-04-18

Browser-based living meta-analysis apps spanning 19 cardiology drug classes. Each app ships a full Protocol → Search → Screening → Extraction → Analysis → Scientific Output pipeline, with auto-seeded canonical trials and pooled estimates cross-checked against published meta-analyses.

Every app tile below opens the full RapidMeta workbench — click into Screening to see Included / Excluded decisions, Extraction for 2×2 tables, and Analysis Suite for forest plots, Bayesian posterior, and PRISMA 2020 flow.
