Living meta-analysis apps — 77 therapeutic topics, validated against published pools

Mahmood Ahmad · Pairwise · Updated 2026-04-20

Browser-based living meta-analysis apps spanning 77 therapeutic topics across cardiology, neurology (stroke thrombolysis + 3 thrombectomy topics + epilepsy: CBD and fenfluramine in Dravet/LGS), oncology (breast, prostate, lung, haematology), gastroenterology, hepatology, respiratory, dermatology (including JAKi in AD), endocrinology (including tirzepatide 15 mg for obesity), infectious disease, vaccinology, rheumatology, ophthalmology, psychiatry, and women's health. This is the final pairwise batch before transition to network meta-analysis (NMA) apps. Each app ships a full Protocol → Search → Screening → Extraction → Analysis → Scientific Output pipeline, with auto-seeded canonical trials and pooled estimates cross-checked against published meta-analyses.

Every app tile below opens the full RapidMeta workbench — click into Screening to see Included / Excluded decisions, Extraction for 2×2 tables, and Analysis Suite for forest plots, Bayesian posterior, and PRISMA 2020 flow. Each app links to its frozen PROSPERO-style protocol (v1.0, frozen 2026-04-19) in the header toolbar.
