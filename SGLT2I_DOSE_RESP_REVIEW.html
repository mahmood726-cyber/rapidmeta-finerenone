SGLT2 inhibitor dose vs HbA1c and hospitalization for heart failure — pooled dose-response meta-analysis

Pooled class-level dose-response re-analysis of 3 SGLT2i multi-dose trials: Wilding 2009 (PMID 19196325) · EMPA-REG OUTCOME (PMID 26378978) · CANVAS Program (PMID 28605608).

Methods (click to expand)

This review pools 3 SGLT2 inhibitor trials with within-trial multi-dose data:

  1. Wilding 2009 (Diabetes Obes Metab): dapagliflozin 2.5/5/10/20/50 mg vs placebo; 12-week Phase 2 dose-ranging.
  2. EMPA-REG OUTCOME 2015 (Zinman et al., NEJM): empagliflozin 10 mg vs 25 mg vs placebo; CV-outcomes trial in T2DM with CV disease.
  3. CANVAS Program 2017 (Neal et al., NEJM): canagliflozin 100 mg vs 300 mg vs placebo; pooled CANVAS + CANVAS-R analysis.

class-equivalence caveat: Doses are pooled at the raw mg scale across 3 different drugs (dapagliflozin, empagliflozin, canagliflozin). The dose axis is therefore drug-dependent; 10 mg means very different things across drugs. The pool implicitly assumes equipotent dose-response on the per-mg scale, which is methodologically debatable. A %-of-max-dose harmonization or per-drug separate analysis would each be defensible alternatives; this flagship chose raw mg for pedagogical simplicity and to match the engine input shape.

Limited evidence base: SGLT2i CVOTs are overwhelmingly single-dose. Only 3 trials contribute within-trial multi-dose data here. k = 3 (HbA1c) and k = 2 (hHF) trigger the engine's coverage_warning.

Statistical methods match the engine v0.2.0 conventions: two-stage Greenland-Longnecker linear pool with REML+HKSJ (HKSJ floor max(1, Q/(k-1))); restricted cubic splines with Harrell-default knots; Wald non-linearity test under diagonal-PM v0.1 (engine non-linearity p will diverge from R full-REML — see R-parity tab); one-stage hierarchical (hHF, binary, lme4::glmer) and weighted-linear (HbA1c, continuous, lme4::lmer) computed by R and surfaced from precomputed JSON. PI uses tk-1 df (Cochrane Handbook v6.5).

Continuous-outcome support: Engine v0.2.0 adds a continuous-outcome branch in fitLinear/fitRCS using a per-trial covariance matrix that mirrors the binary GL shared-reference structure (off-diagonal = sdref2/nref; diagonal = sdi2/ni + sdref2/nref). Validated against R dosresmeta(type="md") in the R-parity badge tab.

F-1 zero-cell continuity correction: Engine v0.2.0 conditionally adds 0.5 to events and 1.0 to n in BOTH reference and contrast arms ONLY when ≥1 cell is zero (per advanced-stats.md: unconditional correction biases OR→1). Triggered on the hHF tab if any arm has 0 events.
