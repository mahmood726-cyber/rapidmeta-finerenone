## 3. Ranking (SUCRA)

Abrocitinib ≈ Upadacitinib (top two) → Lebrikizumab → Dupilumab → Nemolizumab ≈ Baricitinib → Tralokinumab → Placebo.

**Teaching point for students:** The **oral JAK1 inhibitors (Abro, Upa)** achieve the highest EASI-75 responder rates but carry class-level safety signals (MACE, VTE, malignancy — 2021 FDA boxed warning for all JAKis extrapolated from tofacitinib ORAL-Surveillance). The **IL-13 mAbs (Dupi, Lebri)** offer lower numerical efficacy but a more favourable safety profile and SC dosing. **Tralokinumab's lower OR** partly reflects trial design differences (single-drug monotherapy stricter than combination ECZTRA-2).
